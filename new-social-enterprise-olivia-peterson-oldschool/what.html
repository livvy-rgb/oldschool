WHAT WE DO

In New Zealand, our generations are living longer than ever than ever but are lonelier than ever.




Oldschool is a certified educational programme created to bridge a connection between the loneliest generations of NZ, our young adults and our elderly community.




Oldschool is run through secondary schools throughout NZ, where students are given the opportunity to complete a specifically designed programme to work together with our elderly communities to help combat the loneliness epidemic we are facing in NZ and the negative physical and mental effects loneliness has on our young and old people.




Specialized interactive activities and resources such as learning how to build meaningful relationships, learning valuable social skills, sharing skills, and bringing awareness to the loneliness epidemic, have been designed to ensure the most is ganied out of this experience for both parties involved.




Be a part of Oldschool today through your school making, learning less lonely.

THE OLDSCHOOL PROGRAMMES

Our programmes are specially designed to fit your secondary schools curriculum and cover many topics that are required for the students to learn.




Our programmes are easy to follow, provide life changing opportunities for students and the elderly community and the school is supported by us throughout.

THE OLDSCHOOL RESOURCES

All of our Oldschool programs come with designed, ready made resources that can be easily followed and run with no hassle and little thought on your part. Oldschool is there to guide you through at all times and have created these resources to ensure your students and elderly community gets the most out of this experience.
