Our Why

Can you recall the feeling of loneliness?
Loneliness is the unfulfilled desire for another persons company.
At its worst loneliness is the emotional equivalent of physical starvation.
Like starvation, loneliness can cause delusional thinking in those who are
experiencing it.

Loneliness is a disease.
Loneliness enters slowly, quietly, with little indication of its potential.
Its early symptoms might be a feeling of unease, like you can’t find your wallet.

But by the time loneliness gets your attention it has already smashed
up your house. And now, you have got to act fast, it means to do you harm.
And that’s our WHY.
We want to use preventative actions to reduce loneliness in those most affected.

89,000 people in our elderly communities and 150,000
15-24 year old’s have often felt lonely in the past 4 weeks and here at Oldschool
we want to change that.

But we need YOU, and your secondary school to help bring about this change.
