WHO WE ARE

At Oldschool, we are a bunch of people passionate about looking after the lonely, all while bringing awareness and working on prevention of loneliness in New Zealand's loneliest generations, 15-24 year olds and the elderly community.




Oldschool works directly with local secondary schools throughout New Zealand and retirement villages to bridge a connection between the loneliest generations of New Zealand.

OLDSCHOOL IS

Educative

Oldschool Value

Oldschool is Educative. We use our skilled team and specifically designed programmes and resources to teach the young and the old all about loneliness and what we can do to help those who suffer from loneliness.

Purposeful

Oldschool Value

Oldschool is Purposeful and everything we do has a purpose behind it. We are passionate about our purpose of educating and uniting two of the loneliest generations together to help combat the effects of loneliness.

Intergenerational

Oldschool Value

Oldschool is Intergenerational and we pride ourselves on bridging a connection between the young and the old generations through Oldschool programmes.
