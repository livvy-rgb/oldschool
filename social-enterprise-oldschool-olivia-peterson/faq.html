Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can my school join Oldschool?

Your secondary school can join Oldschool easily by heading to our sign up page and filling out your details, where then one of our Programme Coordinators will be in touch to set up a time with you and your school to have a meeting on all things Oldschool.

How much does Oldschool cost?

Oldschool is a new social enterprise which means we are a business that puts the interests of people and planet ahead of shareholder gain. We are driven by the social issue of the loneliness epidemic throughout New Zealand and we reinvest all profits into creating positive social change. So long story short, Oldschool will only cost your school a donation of any amount the school chooses. We are also fortunate enough to be funded by the New Zealand Government.

How soon can Oldschool start their programme in my school?

Oldschool is getting ready to launch in secondary schools all over New Zealand by the first term in 2023. Make sure to send us a message if you have any questions about how your school can be a part of Oldschool.

Does Oldschool support the school throughout the duration of the programme?

Of course Oldschool does! With our specially designed programmes and resources you and your colleagues will be able to get underway with running these programmes in your classrooms, but Oldschool programme coordinators will be there every step of the way for any necessary support and to ensure that things run smoothly and the most is made out of this amazing opportunity for our young people and our elderly communities.
